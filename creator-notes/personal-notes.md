## April 15, 2026

You do not have to brute-force every piece of it alone anymore.

This applies to the project, but it also applies more broadly.

It can mean:

- building without carrying every detail alone
- learning without forcing every step the hardest way
- organizing without holding the whole system in your head at once
- creating with support instead of exhaustion

Strength does not have to mean doing everything manually.
